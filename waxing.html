Waxing

It is a highly effective, semi-permanent hair removal method that extracts hair from the root using hot or cold wax, typically resulting in smooth skin for 3-6 weeks. Key benefits include, slower/finer regrowth,, reduced irritation (no razor burn), and built-in skin exfoliation.

Benefits Of Waxing

  • ✔ Exfoliation Benefits
  • ✔ Finer/Slower Regrowth
  • ✔ Reduced Skin Irritation
